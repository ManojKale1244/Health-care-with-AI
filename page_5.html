Physical Therapy

Physical therapy helps individuals recover strength, mobility, and function after injury or surgery through personalized exercises.

Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapy focuses on enabling people to participate in daily activities by improving their physical, cognitive, and motor skills.

Speech Therapy

Speech therapy assists individuals in overcoming speech, language, and swallowing difficulties through targeted exercises and techniques.
